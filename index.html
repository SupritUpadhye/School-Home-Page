Welcome to Span international academy. Meet the school of your dreams. The learning centre that is more than just an academic institute.

Span international academy is an instantly affable school for Gen Z. The location, in-house atmosphere and well-balanced curriculum make a student ‘feel at home’. Dilawar - Grade 9

My name is Dilawar Parkar and I have been studying in this school since grade 3 and now I am in grade 9!!! I found it so easy to make new friends when I first came to Span International Academy, students and teachers both made me feel so welcome. I have learned valuable leadership skills while learning at my own pace. Span International Academy integrates compassion into its teaching standards, emphasizes the importance of values, and builds leaders through each of its classes and lessons. When I arrived at SIA, my English ability was extremely limited. I found it very difficult to understand what was going on in lessons. The teachers are constantly encouraging me to do my best. It is great knowing that they are there to help me reach my full potential. Our teachers are passionate about seeing their students succeed; they are so great about sharing moments of pride they see within us.
